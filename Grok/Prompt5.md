### Quick Evaluation of Gemini's Recommendation
**Strengths of TMD Moiré (Transition Metal Dichalcogenides like WSe₂/WS₂):**
- **Strong correlations and flat bands**: They host Mott insulators, antiferromagnetic states, and superconducting domes tunable by twist angle (~3.5–5° range), displacement field, and filling. This is a cleaner platform for studying high-Tc-like phenomenology than early MATBG in some ways.
- **Phonon and Seebeck synergy**: TMDs have strong spin-orbit coupling, valley physics, native bandgaps, and tunable moiré phonons (electric-field tunable flat/tunable phonon dispersions). Good for your "crystal phonon lattice gates" and thermoelectric/Seebeck integration for thermal management or hybrid energy harvesting.
- **Topological/hybrid potential**: Supports chiral superconductivity, possible topological features, and easier integration with dielectrics or other layers for quantum capacitance/supercapacitor behavior.

**Limitations for Room-Temp**:
